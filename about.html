About Us

Our Story

Growing up, I didn't get to watch Neil Armstrong and Buzz Aldrin make history. I didn't get to watch the Apollo 11 Moon Landing. But this hasn't stopped me from pursuing what I love. I've always been fascinated by our the stars and planets and galaxy, and luckily, my interests have passed on to my children. Without a doubt, my chilren are my world. I've combined my two passions to create HyperSpace so that every child in our community will have a fun and safe place to explore the world.
