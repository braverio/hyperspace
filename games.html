Games

Test your skills with the various games that HyperSpace offers. Team up with your friends or make new ones as you trench across the galaxy! Ticket payouts vary with each game.

Shoot the Alien

Stand from a distance and use your space gun to try to hunt down the galactic bounty hunters! The aliens will move in various directions, so be determined!

ASTERIODS: 6

Toss Into Space

Try to land your spaceship onto the various planets. If you fail, then you'll be lost in space forever! Your crew is counting on you. Their fate rests in your hands.

ASTERIODS: 4

Basketball

Do you think you can ball? Test your skills by shooting as many hoops as you can in just one minute. Compete against your friends to claim the title of the true basketball champion!

ASTERIODS: 8

Skeeball

This classic will have you coming back for more! Try to place as many balls as you can into the various circles. Will you score 50, 100, 200 points? Try with all your might!

ASTERIODS: 10

Air Hockey

Who doesn't love the classic game of hockey? Pair up with your friend, neighbor, or stranger to defend your planet from the attacks of asteroids. Direct them towards the enemy's base to defend your land!

ASTERIODS: 8

Crossy Asteroid Belt

Based off the acclaimed Crossy Road, in this controller-arcade game, try to land your spaceship across the Asteroid Belt. Avoid getting crushed in the process and saving as much fuel as possible.

ASTERIODS: 10

Spaceship Racing

In this race simulation, pair off against the computer or a friend or a rival to hyperspeed nyour way across the finish line. Your reward? Tickets. Lots of tickets. What are you waiting for? Buckle up!

ASTERIODS: 10

Asteroid Belt Maze

Find your way through our real-life 1000ft maze. Can you find the way out or will you be trapped within the black vortex forever?
